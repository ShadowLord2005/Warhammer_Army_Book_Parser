Whilst the Galadhrim prefer to don golden armour and act as a fighting force, some Wood Elves prefer to utilise stealth to combat their foes. Acting as scouts, these Elves patrol the boundaries of Lothlórien for any interlopers into their realm, and act swiftly and silently to dispatch them. Using their Elven cloaks, the Wood Elves blend in with their surroundings to ambush their foes, with many Orc bands being felled without even knowing they were being tracked.
