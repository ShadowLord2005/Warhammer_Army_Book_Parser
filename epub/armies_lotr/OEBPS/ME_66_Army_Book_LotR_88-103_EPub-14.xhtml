Additional Rules

A Riders of Théoden Army must always contain Théoden, who is always the Army’s General.

Riders of Rohan do not count towards this Army’s Bow Limit.

Special Rules

“Ride for ruin and the world’s ending” – Such is the ferocity in which the Rohirrim charge, that those who attempt to stand against them will be flattened where they stand.

Friendly Rohan Cavalry models gain a bonus of +1 to their Strength on a turn in which they Charge.

“Death!” – The battle cry of the King of Rohan inspires all those around him to great deeds of valour – even if it will be their doom.

Once per game, at the start of any Fight Phase, Théoden can use this special rule so long as he is alive and on the battlefield. If he does, then each friendly Rohan Hero within 12" of Théoden can declare a free Heroic Combat or a free Heroic Strike (if able) during the declare Heroic Actions Step. This is treated as an Active ability belonging to Théoden.

“Forth Eorlingas!” – The Rohirrim will stoically defend their liege and his kin against all that stand against them.

Friendly Cavalry Warrior models within 3" of either Théoden or Éomer gain the Dominant (2) special rule.
