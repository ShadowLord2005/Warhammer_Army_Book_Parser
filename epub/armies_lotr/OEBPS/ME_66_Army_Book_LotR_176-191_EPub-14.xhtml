Within many of the caverns and tunnels of the Misty Mountains can be found the Cave Trolls; lumbering and loathsome creatures that seek the darkness of caves. These great beasts often fight alongside the likes of Moria Goblins, their great strength the perfect complement to the teeming Goblin hordes, smashing aside any foe too tough for the Goblins to crack; their thick leathery hides protecting them from incoming blows as they do so.
