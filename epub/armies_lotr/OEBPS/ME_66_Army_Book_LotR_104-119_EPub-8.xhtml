Additional Rules

A Reclamation of Osgiliath Army must include Boromir, Captain of the White Tower, who is always the Army’s General.

Special Rules

“Remember today, little brother” – The sons of Denethor have fought alongside one another since childhood and can easily anticipate the other’s movements on the battlefield.

While Boromir and Faramir are within 3" of each other, they may spend each other’s Might, Will and Fate points as if they were their own.

“This city was once the jewel of our kingdom” – Though it was once a city of splendour, Osgiliath now lies in ruins. Those that fight within its crumbling streets will need to keep a steady footing.

Friendly models gain the Mountain Dweller special rule.

“For Gondor!” – With a rousing cry, Boromir can inspire the armies of Gondor to press the attack and fight with all they can muster to deliver victory.

Once per game, at the start of any Fight Phase, Boromir can use this special rule so long as he is alive and on the battlefield. If he does, then friendly Gondor Warrior models within 6" of Boromir gain a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes until the end of the turn. This is treated as an Active ability belonging to Boromir.
