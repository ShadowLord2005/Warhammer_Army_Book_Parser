Rider of the Dead .....................25 points

Just as they acted as the vanguard of the army in life, the riders of the dead do so again in death. Riding out ahead of their allies upon ghastly steeds, these dread apparitions are enough to chill the souls of their enemies and cause utter panic within the ranks of those who they charge. Much like their counterparts on foot, the weapons of these ghostly riders are spectral in nature, and care not for plate or mail as they find their mark.
