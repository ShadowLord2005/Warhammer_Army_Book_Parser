Special Rules

The Fleetmaster – As the Corsairs sail towards battle on the Pelennor, they are commanded by their ruthless fleetmaster who demands the utmost loyalty from his crew.

The Corsair Captain that is your General increases their Attacks and Wounds to 3 each, and gains the Fearless and Sharpshooter special rules.

The Last Stroke of Doom – The Corsairs are a barbarous bunch and they will show no mercy upon those who they manage to corner as they raid the lands of Gondor.

Friendly Corsair models gain the Backstabbers special rule.

Ballistae Assault – Before the Corsairs leap into battle, they will bombard their targets with bolts from the ballistae on their ships.

After both sides have been deployed, but before the first turn of the game, a single Corsair Ballista may make a shooting attack following all the normal rules for doing so.
