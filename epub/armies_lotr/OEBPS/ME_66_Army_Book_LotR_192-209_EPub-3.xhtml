Haradrim Chieftain ...............50 points

Each Haradrim tribe is presided over by their own chieftain, Men who have used their own cunning to rise to power within their tribe. The power of these chieftains is measured in the amount of land they control and the number of people within their tribe. Yet every chieftain is constantly looking to expand their territory, and will readily attack other settlements in a bid to slay a rival chieftain and seize their lands for their own.
