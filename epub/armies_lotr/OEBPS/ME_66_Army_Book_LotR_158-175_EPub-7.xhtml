Uruk-hai Scout Captain .........55 points

At the head of each band of Isengard Scouts run their captains. Formidable warriors that relentlessly pursue their quarry, they do not stop until they have either slain or captured them. When the Uruk-hai Scouts were sent to track down the Fellowship, it was these captains that led the pursuit to Amon Hen; and when Saruman commanded the settlements of Rohan be raided, it was the Uruk-hai Scout Captains who led the assault.
