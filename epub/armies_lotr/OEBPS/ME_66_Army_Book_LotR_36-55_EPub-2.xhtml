Unsteady Nerves Active – Aldor is an old man; holding an arrow drawn back for a length of time can take its toll, and can lead to Aldor loosing an arrow earlier than intended.

Aldor must always shoot first during the Shoot Phase, and will shoot even before Heroic Shoots are resolved. However, Aldor may not declare a Heroic Shoot himself.

Lucky Shot Active – Whether it’s through the benefit of years of practice, or more likely through sheer dumb luck, Aldor will often find himself hitting his target.

Aldor must re-roll failed To Hit Rolls and failed To Wound Rolls when making a shooting attack.

Aldor is one of the many ageing Men of Rohan that must take up arms at the siege of Helm’s Deep. Yet Aldor is far past his prime, having seen many winters across his long lifetime. His strength and aim is also not what it once was, and Aldor struggles to draw his bow back and hold it steady, often resulting in him loosing an arrow before he is ready. However, these arrows seem to regularly find their mark, though this is likely more to do with sheer luck than actual skill.
