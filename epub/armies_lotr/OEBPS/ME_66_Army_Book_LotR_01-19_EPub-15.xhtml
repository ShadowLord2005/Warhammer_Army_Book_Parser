Gimli accompanied his father, Glóin, in attending the Council of Elrond, and joined the Fellowship of the Ring as a representative of the Dwarves. Brash, stubborn and hot-headed, Gimli is a warrior of exceptional martial prowess, and more than capable of fighting off hordes of enemies with a grim determination. Though he harbours a resentment of the Elves, Gimli has struck up a strong friendship with Legolas, one born out of healthy competition about who can slay the most foes in battle – and he fights all the harder to out-do the pointy-eared Elvish princeling!

Special Rules

Axes of the Dwarves! Active – Armed with various types of axe, Gimli is more than capable of unleashing the fury of his race upon his foes.

At the beginning of each Combat he is involved in, Gimli can choose to either fight with an axe in each hand, in which case he will fight with 3 Attacks, or with his two-handed axe.

“I myself am sitting pretty on 43” Active – Legolas and Gimli both wish to prove themselves as the better fighter, and will often engage in friendly competition upon the battlefield.

If your Army contains both Legolas Greenleaf and Gimli, son of Glóin, then keep track of how many enemy models each of them has killed. Whilst he has fewer kills than Legolas, Gimli gains a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes.
