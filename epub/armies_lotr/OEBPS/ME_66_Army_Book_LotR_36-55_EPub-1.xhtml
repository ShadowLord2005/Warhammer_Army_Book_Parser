Háma, Captain of Rohan ..............60 points

Háma is the captain of Théoden’s palace guard and the doorward of Meduseld. It is Háma’s duty to command the king’s personal bodyguards and to ensure that any who approach Théoden do so without means to harm the king. Though only able to watch and obey as his lord fell into despair from the influence of Saruman, Háma places his trust in Gandalf that the Wizard can free Théoden from his shackles and return his liege to his former self, and usher in the renewal of the kingdom of Rohan.

Wargear

Heavy armour and hand weapon.

Special Rules

Leader (Rohan Royal Guard), Sworn Protector (Théoden)

Doorward of Meduseld Passive – Háma and the Royal Guard he commands are tasked with ensuring Théoden’s protection and guarding his lands against intruders.

So long as Théoden is alive and on the battlefield, Háma and any Rohan Royal Guard within 6" of him count as having the Dominant (2) special rule.
