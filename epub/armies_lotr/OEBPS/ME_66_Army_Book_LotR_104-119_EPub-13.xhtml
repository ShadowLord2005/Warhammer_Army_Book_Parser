Defenders of the Pelennor

The Dark Lord Sauron has amassed his armies in Mordor, gathering to his side thousands upon thousands of Orcs and evil Men, and is now ready to unleash his full might upon the lands that oppose him. His hammer blow shall fall swiftly and impact hardest against the kingdom of Gondor; it is upon the fields of the Pelennor where much of the future of Middle-earth will be decided, for good or ill.

Within Minas Tirith, the forces of Gondor prepare themselves for a war unlike any they have ever seen before. Every able-bodied warrior is called out to fight, though they are far outnumbered by the forces of Mordor that have amassed on the fields outside their gates. Led by the White Wizard, the armies of Minas Tirith are now faced with the greatest army of the Third Age – yet they do not face this threat alone…

From the north arrives the Riders of Rohan, led by King Théoden who has come to honour the alliance that once stood between the two kingdoms. And from the south, Aragorn has arrived with his companions, Legolas and Gimli, and perhaps the deadliest army in all of Middle-earth in the spectral form of the Army of the Dead. Even with the lower levels of Minas Tirith overrun with Orcs, the arrival of their allies sees the turning of their fortunes in the war with Mordor.

Together Men of Gondor, Rohan, and those long since dead fight to drive back the armies of Mordor and to ensure that the White City does not fall. Though the lives of many will be claimed in the city’s defence, those who do fall will be remembered as heroes who fought in the greatest battle of the Third Age, and stood up for what is right in the face of a dreadful and unrelenting darkness.

Additional Rules

Rohan Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Rohan Hero.

Gondor Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Gondor Hero or Gandalf the White.

Dunharrow Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Dunharrow Hero, or in the Warband of Aragorn, Legolas or Gimli.

Special Rules

Defend the White City – Those that fought at the Pelennor have but a single goal – to ensure the White City does not fall.

Each Hero in your Army can, once per game, declare a Heroic Combat for free.
