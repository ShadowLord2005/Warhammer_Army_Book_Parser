Not all the Rohirrim are loyal to the King of Rohan. When Lord Thorne betrayed Helm Hammerhand and joined Wulf in attacking Edoras, a band of his followers joined Thorne in settling alight the city. These Rohirrim traitors gladly turned their back on their king, having been promised a wealth of gold and riches for their betrayal. Every bit as skilled as those that remained loyal to Rohan, the traitors have been turned by the poisoned words and half-truths spread by Lord Thorne, and now seek to see Edoras in flames.

Special Rules

Hatred (Rohan)

Spear Mastery Active – Throwing spears are a common weapon in Rohan, and the Rohirrim have become adept at using them both up close and from afar.

This model may use its throwing spears as a normal spear during the Fight Phase, so long as it hasn’t made a shooting attack with it in either the preceding Move Phase or Shoot Phase.
