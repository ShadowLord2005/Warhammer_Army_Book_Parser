The Easterlings are a proud warrior race from the land of Rhûn; a land virtually unmapped by the Men of the West. Fanatically devoted to the Dark Lord and his cause, these zealous warriors are trained in the art of warfare from as soon as they are able to hold a blade. Each Easterling warrior is a skilled fighter and is trained to show no mercy on the battlefield, ruthlessly slaughtering any who would attempt to surrender. Whilst the likes of Orcs may quiver and flee should the battle turn ill, Easterlings will stand and fight until the bitter end – standing stoically in their golden armour.

Within the culture of the Easterlings of Rhûn, nothing is more important than the ability to wield a blade. A strong martial code runs deep within the veins of all Easterlings, and those who rise to the rank of captain are revered throughout the armies of the east. Ruthless, unwavering and highly skilled with a blade, these captains are exemplars of everything that the Easterlings desire in their warriors. Though they may be callous and cruel leaders, their followers will fight for their captains no matter the odds, for none will want to feel their captain’s wrath should they fail.

Special Rules

Phalanx Active – The Easterlings are constantly drilled in the art of war. They can fight in tightly-knit formations and wield bladed pikes with ease.

A model with this special rule can use a pike and a shield at the same time without penalty. Additionally, should an Infantry model with this special rule lose a Combat, up to two friendly models with this special rule can Make Way instead of one.
