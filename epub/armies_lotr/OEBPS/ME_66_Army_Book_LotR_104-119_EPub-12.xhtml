Additional Rules

A Return of the King Army must include Aragorn (Strider) and the King of the Dead. Aragorn is always the Army’s General.

Special Rules

The Heir of Isildur – As the rightful heir to the throne of Gondor, Aragorn has finally come to fulfil his destiny.

Aragorn does not have to pay any points for Andúril, Flame of the West; he automatically has this upgrade – it is free.

“Fight for us and regain your honour” – It is only by fighting alongside the King of Gondor that the dead can be free of their curse and finally be at peace.

Friendly Dunharrow Warrior models within 3" of Aragorn gain a bonus of +1 to their Fight Value.

Visions of Terror – The sight of the King of the Dead charging headlong into the fray is truly horrifying, one that may send enemies fleeing in terror.

Whenever the King of the Dead Charges into Combat, after the Charge has been completed, he may spend a Will Point to use this special rule. If he does, enemy models within 3" of the King of the Dead must take a Courage Test in an order chosen by the King of the Dead’s controlling player. If the test is failed, the model must Move up to its Move Value directly away from the King of the Dead.
