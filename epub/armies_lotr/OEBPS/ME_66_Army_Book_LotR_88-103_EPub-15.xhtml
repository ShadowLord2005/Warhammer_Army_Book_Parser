Army of Edoras

During the reign of Helm Hammerhand, the kingdom of Rohan is at relative peace. The revolts of the Hill Tribes have been quashed, and Orc raids have become few and far between. Yet the standing armies of Rohan remain well trained and ready for war – for peace is a delicate thing and it doesn’t take much for it to shatter under the right circumstances – something Helm discovers following the death of Freca and the war his son, Wulf, brings upon Rohan.

Helm leads the armies of Rohan in defence of its capital of Edoras. A strong and noble warrior and a veteran of many battles, Helm leads his army from the front. At his side ride his two sons, Haleth and Háma, both capable fighters trained in the art of war and skilled with both blade and bow. The sight of the king and his heirs striding into the thick of the fighting without fear is a sight that inspires their warriors to steel themselves against any foe.

Army of Edoras

The enormity of Wulf’s army requires that every able-bodied Rohirrim must fight for the defence of Edoras. With the city ablaze around her, Helm’s daughter, Héra, takes up her blade alongside Olwyn, an ageing Shieldmaiden, and Lief, an esquire of the king, each of them stoically fending off the armies raised by Wulf as they give their all to defend Helm’s kingdom from ruin.

The armies of Edoras are made up of the best warriors the kingdom of Rohan has to offer. Their warriors are hardy folk who have pledged themselves to fight for their liege and his royal line against any foe that would threaten to raze their homeland to the ground. Should Helm or his heirs call for the Rohirrim to redouble their efforts, then the warriors and riders of Rohan will fight with every ounce of strength and fury they can muster, without a moment’s hesitation; and it would take an army at least twice their size to hope to overcome them.
