Corsair Fleets

The Corsairs of Umbar are well known for their skill at seafaring, though even more so for their cut-throat nature and raids upon the coastal cities of Middle-earth. The black-sailed ships of Umbar are a sight that many who live within reach of the sea have begun to recognise all too well, and their presence is enough to instil fear and dread in those whose settlements are about to be ransacked.

A Corsair raid is a swift yet merciless affair. The ship’s ballistae launch bolt after bolt upon the settlement being raided, providing cover for the Corsairs to disembark and race towards their target. Buildings are torched, riches are stolen and any who attempt to stand defiant against the Corsairs are either cut down where they stand or dragged back to the ships to be slaughtered in cold blood in whatever creative ways the captain comes up with.

At the head of the Corsair fleets are the captains of the ships. Ruthless and barbaric men who have seized command of a ship of their own, commanding those on board in the raids on the settlements of Gondor, and demanding the greatest share of plunder. At their side stand their Bo’suns – burly, savage warriors who are tasked with quashing any mutinies, and are well paid for their troubles. Though it is these captains that command the Corsairs, it is often suspected that they themselves are being directed by others in the shadows to some greater end.

It is these fleets that Sauron intends to unleash upon the city of Minas Tirith, paying them well to sail up the Anduin and attack the White City from the port of Harlond. Many of the black-sailed ships make their way up the river, raiding and pillaging as they go. Should the Corsairs reach the city of Minas Tirith, it will likely result in its doom.
