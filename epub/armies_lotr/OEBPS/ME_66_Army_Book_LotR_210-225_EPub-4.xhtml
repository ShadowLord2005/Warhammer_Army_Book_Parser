Additional Rules

A Minas Morgul Army must always contain the Witch-king of Angmar, who is always the Army’s General.

Special Rules

The Dead City – Minas Morgul is a city corrupted by the evil of the Witch-king, twisted and distorted into a place of spectres; and the Orcs that make up its armies seem to have inherited a portion of their ghostly powers.

Mordor Orc Captains and Mordor Orc Warriors gain the Blades of the Dead special rule.

Call of Morgul – The power of the dead city mirrors that of the Nazgûl themselves, drawing the Ringbearer to it should they stray too near to its walls.

If the enemy Army contains a Ringbearer, they will suffer a-1 penalty when rolling for Sauron’s Will.

Lead through fear – The Orcs of Minas Morgul are more afraid of the Witch-king and his spectral allies than they are of any foe.

Friendly Orc models within 3" of a friendly Spirit model automatically pass Courage Tests when attempting to Charge a model with the Terror special rule.
