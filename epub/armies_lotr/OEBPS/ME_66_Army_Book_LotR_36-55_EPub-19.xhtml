Upon reaching the White City, Pippin pledges his service to Denethor in payment for Boromir saving his life and joins the ranks of the Citadel Guard. It is during the battle for Minas Tirith where it becomes apparent that there is little left of the impetuous Hobbit who set off from the Shire. Instead, Pippin has become a courageous and valuable ally, earning the respect of those within Minas Tirith.
