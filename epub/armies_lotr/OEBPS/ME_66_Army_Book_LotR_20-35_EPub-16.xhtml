One of the chief advisors of King Théoden, Gamling has been a captain in the royal guard for many years. Gamling possesses a quiet wisdom, providing sage advice and counsel to his liege in times of need. In battle, Gamling is given the honour of carrying the Royal Standard of Rohan into battle, tasked with ensuring that the colours remain flying to inspire the Rohirrim onwards. Ever at the side of his liege, Gamling will fight aside Théoden at all times, sharing in his king’s every danger.
