Haldir, Galadhrim Captain........70 points

Haldir is one of the most trusted and able captains within the ranks of the Galadhrim, tasked with defending the borders of the Golden Wood. As an expert marksman, Haldir is perhaps the best archer within Lothlórien, able to loose arrows far quicker than the majority of his kin. It is Haldir who leads the army of the Galadhrim that march to aid those in Helm’s Deep, honouring the alliance that once stood between Men and Elves. Though he fights valiantly, the battle will claim Haldir’s life; nevertheless, his sacrifice will not be in vain, and the forces he brings to Helm’s Deep will be instrumental in its defence.
