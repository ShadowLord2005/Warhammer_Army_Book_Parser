Additional Rules

A Riders of Éomer Army must include Éomer, who is always the Army’s General.

Riders of Rohan do not count towards this Army’s Bow Limit.

Special Rules

Look to the sun – The arrival of the White Wizard and the Rohirrim brings with it the morning sun, blinding their foes as they charge into battle.

Once per game, at the start of any friendly Activation Phase, Gandalf can use this special rule so long as he is alive and on the battlefield. If he does, then until the end of the turn, enemy models Engaged in Combat with a friendly Cavalry model that made a Charge that turn, or Supporting a Combat against a friendly Cavalry model that made a Charge that turn, suffer a -1 penalty to their Duel Roll.

“Ride for ruin and the world’s ending” – Such is the ferocity in which the Rohirrim charge, that those who attempt to stand against them will be flattened where they stand.

Friendly Rohan Cavalry models gain a bonus of +1 to their Strength on a turn in which they Charge.

“To the King!” – Éomer is determined to reach Théoden and deliver Helm’s Deep from the onslaught of Uruk-hai that are overwhelming its walls.

Éomer may declare a Heroic Combat each turn for free.
