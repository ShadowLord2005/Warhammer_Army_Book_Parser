King of the Dead ....................100 points

When the armies of Númenor were preparing to go to war with Sauron, the king of the mountains swore an oath to Isildur that he and his people would join their fight; yet when the time came, they refused, resulting in Isildur cursing them to not rest until their oath is fulfilled. Though his body has long been reduced to dust, the king’s spirit lingers on in torment until he honours his word. It is the coming of Aragorn that has finally provided the opportunity, and the King of the Dead has pledged to join the heir of Isildur and take up arms against the armies of Mordor – the oath sworn to Isildur will finally be fulfilled and the dead will be able to rest in peace.

Special Rules

Blades of the Dead, Spectral Walk, Terror

Drain Soul Active – The dread powers harnessed by the King of the Dead are powerful enough to drain the essence of his victims.

A model that suffers a Wound from the King of the Dead in Combat, which is not then prevented, will automatically have their Wounds reduced to 0, causing them to be slain and removed as a casualty.

The Dead and the Living Passive – The King of the Dead has no power over the living and can only command those under the effects of the same curse as himself.

Only friendly Dunharrow models may benefit from the King of the Dead’s Stand Fast or benefit from his Heroic Actions.
