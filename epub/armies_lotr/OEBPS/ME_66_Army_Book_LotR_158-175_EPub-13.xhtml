An Orc with a shrewder cunning than most of those in the ranks of Isengard’s armies, Sharku is the commander of the Wolves of Isengard. When the Rohirrim attempt to make for the safety of Helm’s Deep, it is Sharku who is tasked with hunting them down and killing them on the road, an act he takes great relish in performing. Few amongst Orc-kind can ride a Warg as well as Sharku, and those who find themselves on the receiving end of a charge from Sharku and his Warg are only a swift snap of the jaws away from an unpleasant demise.
