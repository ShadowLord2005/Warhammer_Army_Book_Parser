A sneaky and vindictive Orc who was never one for following orders, Snaga was part of the Orc band sent by Sauron to keep an eye on his puppet, Saruman. Along with Grishnákh and his Orcs, Snaga met with Uglúk’s scouts following their capture of Merry and Pippin to ensure the Halflings reached Isengard. Yet, having not eaten properly in days, Snaga’s mind turned towards eating their prisoners – something that ultimately results in Snaga becoming the main course.

Grishnákh leads the Orcs sent from Minas Morgul to keep an eye on Saruman’s progress. A ruthless and calculating Orc, Grishnákh has risen through the Orcish ranks by honing his skills over years of combat and the raiding of settlements. Grishnákh shares his followers’ desire to feast upon their Hobbit captives, and when the Orcs and Uruk-hai are slaughtered by Éomer’s Éored, Grishnákh follows Merry and Pippin into Fangorn – where he meets an end befitting of an Orcish intruder…
