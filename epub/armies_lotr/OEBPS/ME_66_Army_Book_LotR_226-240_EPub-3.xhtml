Assault Upon Helm’s Deep

It is at the Siege of Helm’s Deep where the realms of Men feel the full force of the armies of Isengard. Rank upon rank of fearsome Uruk-hai bear down upon the fortress, each of them driven forwards by their unrelenting desire to see those within Helm’s Deep slaughtered and the walls of the fortress stained with the blood of Men and Elves alike. Driven into a frenzy by the words of their master, the fighting Uruk-hai are a force unlike any that has stood outside the great stronghold of the Rohirrim.

Using huge siege ladders to scale the walls of Helm’s Deep, the Uruk-hai clamber up them with great athleticism and swarm the walls of the fortress. Clad in thick armour and wielding weapons forged in the pits of Isengard, the Uruk-hai fight like warriors possessed, unflinching as they swing their weapons with reckless abandon. Some Uruk-hai are whipped into a bloodthirsty frenzy, donning little to no armour and carrying huge blades that can cleave an enemy in two, or force them over the edge of the walls to the ground below.

Commanding the assault upon Helm’s Deep in the absence of the White Wizard are the captains of the Uruk-hai, the strongest and most vicious among the ranks of their kind. There are few who can match these barbaric fighters for sheer power upon a battlefield, and the strength they possess can cleave bone, armour and weaponry with a frightening ease. To come face-to-face with these ruthless killers will result in almost certain death for all who are not highly skilled warriors themselves.

It is not just thousands of Uruk-hai that Saruman has unleashed, for the Uruk-hai bring with them the White Wizard’s most nefarious contraptions of warfare. Huge ballistae loose great bolts towards the walls, shattering stone and raising enormous ladders to the high battlements outside the keep. Teams of Uruk-hai bring demolition charges into battle, detonating them in order to break stone and cause massive casualties. The army of Isengard is one of immense power with a single driving force – to end the world of Men.
