The armies of Mordor are made up of more than just Orcs, with some fortresses garrisoned by scores of ferocious Mordor Uruk-hai. Stronger than Orcs and less prone to fleeing the battlefield, Mordor Uruk-hai pose a serious threat to anyone forced to face them. Their sole purpose is to tear foes apart, either using their cleavers to cut through flesh with huge swinging blows or using their muscular frames to literally rip enemies limb from limb.

Those that captain the Mordor Uruk-hai are the most savage and barbaric of their kind. Huge, snarling beasts with a hunger for flesh, these fearsome warriors fight with a brutality that rivals even the strongest of Mordor’s warriors. On the battlefield these Uruks are a terrible force, swinging their weapons with great cleaving blows that can take a foe’s arm clean off and revelling in the destruction they wrought with a grim malice.
