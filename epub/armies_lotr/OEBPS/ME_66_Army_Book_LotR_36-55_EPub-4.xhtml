Unlike his older brother, Háma is carefree, good-natured, with a poet’s heart. Musically gifted, he would rather sing of epic battles than partake in them. A calmer more sensitive individual, finding joy in the likes of music and poetry rather than practising with a blade. However, this doesn’t make Háma any less of a warrior, and he is still more than capable in his own right and is particularly adept with the use of a bow. When Edoras is put under siege, Háma sees it as his duty to ride with his father and brother in defence of the city, and to help his people escape to safety. He is very close to his younger sister Héra and would do anything to protect her.

Háma, Prince of Rohan ..........70 points

The eldest son of Helm Hammerhand, and heir to the throne of Rohan, Haleth is the most like his father – tall, strong, and a skilled fighter. He is full of life, with a ready laugh. He can also be quick tempered and defensive, especially in his unwavering belief in the invincibility of his father. But he is also courageous and loyal and when tested, readily puts his life on the line. When the armies of Dunland come to bring ruin to Rohan, Haleth takes up arms without hesitation and rides alongside his father to defend his homeland. Few who stand against Haleth can hope to match his exceptional martial skill and it may yet take more cunning methods to fell the prince of Rohan…
