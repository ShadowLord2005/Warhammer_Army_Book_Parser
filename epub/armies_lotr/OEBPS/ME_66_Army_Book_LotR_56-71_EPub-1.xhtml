An experienced veteran within the ranks of Faramir’s rangers, Madril has seen many conflicts over his years in the service of the White Tower. Having long served at the side of his commander, Madril is Faramir’s most trusted captain and takes up command of Osgiliath when Faramir ventures into Ithilien. When the armies of Mordor invade Osgiliath, it is the ambushes set in place by Madril that keeps the onslaught at bay for long enough for many of Gondor’s forces to be able to escape to safety.

Having spent many years fighting alongside Faramir in the wilderness of Ithilien, Damrod has become an experienced ranger in his own right. Blessed with a keen eye, Damrod has proved to be highly accurate with the use of bow and arrow, felling many of Sauron’s minions with an expertly placed shot. When the armies of Mordor advance upon Osgiliath, Damrod takes up sword and bow in defence of his homeland.

Master of Reserves Passive – As a seasoned ranger who has served for many years, Madril has plenty of experience of when it is best to execute an ambush on his foes.

In Scenarios where you roll for Warbands to arrive, or ones where you roll to see which part of the board you deploy a Warband in (though not when rolling to choose deployment zones), you may modify the roll for Madril’s Warband by +1 or -1, even if he is not on the battlefield.
