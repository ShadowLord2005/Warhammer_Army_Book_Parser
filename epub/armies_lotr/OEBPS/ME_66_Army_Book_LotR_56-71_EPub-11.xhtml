Those who broke the oath sworn to Isildur suffered a terrible curse, forcing them to linger on. Over the centuries of the Third Age, their bodies began to decay and slowly turn to dust; yet they did not rest. Instead they became spectral shadows of their former selves, forced to wander their mountain homes in shame. A grim fate awaits any who stray into the realm of the dead. Though their blades have long since lost their edge, the armour of mortal beings holds no defence against them, cutting through their very soul.
