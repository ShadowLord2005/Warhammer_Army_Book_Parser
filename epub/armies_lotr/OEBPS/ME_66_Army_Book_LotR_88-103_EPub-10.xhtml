Army Composition

Additional Rules

A Ride Out Army must always contain Théoden, who is always the Army’s General.

Riders of Rohan do not count towards this Army’s Bow Limit.

Special Rules

“Now for wrath, now for ruin and the red dawn” Determined to ride out in one glorious charge for glory, Théoden and his allies will fight to the very last in defence of Helm’s Deep.

When a friendly Cavalry model Moves, it must Charge if able. Additionally, on a turn in which a friendly Cavalry model Charges, they gain a bonus of +1 to their Strength and may re-roll any 1s during a Duel Roll that turn.

“The horn of Helm Hammerhand shall sound in the deep; one last time” – Whilst those who can, ride out, Gimli climbs the Hornburg to give a great blast on the fabled horn of Helm Hammerhand – a sound that reverberates around the valley.

Once per game, at the start of any Move phase, you can declare you are using this special rule. Until the end of the turn, friendly Cavalry models gain the Terror special rule.
