Legions of Mordor

The Dark Lord Sauron has amassed his armies in Mordor, gathering to his side thousands upon thousands of Orcs and evil Men, and is now ready to unleash his full might upon the lands that oppose him. It is the kingdom of Gondor that is the target for his fury, and it is upon the fields of the Pelennor where the legions of Mordor shall bring their full might to bear against the enemies of the Dark Lord.

A huge army of Orcs the likes of which has never been seen in Middle-earth has been amassed, and is now prepared to assail the White City with the purpose of seeing it in flames and reduced to rubble. Led by the great Orc tactician, Gothmog, these Orcs are the best equipped and most well armed of all the Orcs in the service of Sauron; and they will attack with their full fury to crush the race of Men and usher in the time of the Orc.

Sauron has more than Orcs in his service. From the south marches an army of Haradrim: bands of tribal warriors riding atop great Mûmakil and trampling any who lie in their path. From the lands of Rhûn the Easterlings have joined the Dark Lord’s cause, each a fanatical warrior with a zealous devotion to Sauron and possessing exceptional martial skill. Together, these forces seek to spell the doom of Minas Tirith and those who have pledged their lives to defend it.

Yet in overall command of the legions of Mordor is Sauron’s deadliest and most loyal servant, one who has served his master for centuries and now commands the greatest battle in the Dark Lord’s name – the dreaded Witch-king of Angmar. With such a fearsome foe commanding the legions sent forth from Mordor, it is a dark day for the free peoples of Middle-earth; one which may yet see their lands fall into permanent shadow…
