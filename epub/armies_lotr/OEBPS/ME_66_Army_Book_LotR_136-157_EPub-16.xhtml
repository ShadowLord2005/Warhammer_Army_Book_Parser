Roaming the lands of Mordor are vicious and feral Wargs: bloodthirsty wolf-like creatures with a never-ending hunger. Some wily Orcs have managed to exercise a form of control over these savage beasts, riding them into battle. Packs of Warg Riders scout ahead of the armies of Mordor, rooting out enemy scouts and running down any who stray too far away from their forces with raw animalistic fury.
