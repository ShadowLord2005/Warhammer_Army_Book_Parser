Cirith Ungol

Within the western mountain face of Mordor, near to the fortress of Minas Morgul, lies the high pass of Cirith Ungol, a path into Mordor that is said to contain a nameless terror that will feast upon all who attempt to cross. Upon the other side of the pass is the tower of Cirith Ungol, a fortification garrisoned by Orcs and Mordor Uruk-hai ever watchful should any enemies of the Dark Lord, or even something fouler, try to enter Mordor by that route.

Within the tower of Cirith Ungol are stationed both Orcs and Uruk-hai, each led by cruel and savvy leaders. Gorbag commands the Orcs within Cirith Ungol; an intelligent and savage Orc, Gorbag will stand up to the stronger Uruk-hai should his authority be questioned. Unlike most of his kind, Gorbag knows he can use his situation to his own advantage, and will often use his cunning to convince an unwitting Uruk-hai to act to his own benefit should he need to.

The Mordor Uruk-hai are led by Shagrat, the strongest Uruk within the garrison. Whilst he may not possess the same intellect as his Orc counterpart, Shagrat will crush any resistance to his command with utter cruelty. Though the Orcs and Uruk-hai are stationed to work together, both harbour a great disdain for the other, constantly seeking to undermine their counterpart. With tension on a knife-edge, it wouldn’t take much for the Orcs and Uruk-hai to turn against each other; and should they be forced to fight alongside their rivals, each will seek to outdo the other.

The high pass itself is home to Shelob, the spawn of Ungoliant. This giant spider-like creature is a ravenous being that will eagerly feast upon any unfortunate beings that she would come across, whether they be Orcs, Uruk-hai or perhaps something more appetising. Those who Shelob marks out to be her prey will likely have no idea they are being stalked, only finding out when it is already too late…
