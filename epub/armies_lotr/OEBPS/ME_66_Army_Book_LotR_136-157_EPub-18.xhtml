As the years have passed, new strains of Orcs have been bred within Mordor. Designed to guard the Black Gate, the Morannon Orcs are stronger, tougher and more savage than the Orcs that man Sauron’s other bastions. Clad in thick plated armour rather than rags and leathers, the Morannon Orcs fight in a more orderly fashion than the rabbles of lesser Orcs. It is these Orcs that Sauron has unleashed upon the kingdom of Gondor, for they will stop at nothing to end the race of Men.
