The colossal Mûmakil are the most fearsome weapons the Haradrim have at their disposal, often gifted to their chieftains and lords as a symbol of their power. These great beasts carry the Haradrim to war in the Howdahs constructed on their backs, allowing the Haradrim to ride above the battles raging below and loose arrows upon their enemies from up high. A charging Mûmak is nigh on unstoppable, smashing aside anything that stands in its way with its enormous tusks and trampling any who find themselves in the path of its lumbering frame.
