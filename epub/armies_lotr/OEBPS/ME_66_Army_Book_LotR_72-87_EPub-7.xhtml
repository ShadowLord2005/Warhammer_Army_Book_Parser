The woodland realm of Lothlórien is defended first and foremost by the warriors of the Galadhrim, steadfast warriors who have honed their skills over the centuries. Like all Elves, the Galadhrim are masters with sword, spear and bow, able to use all manner of weapons to fend off their foes. Tasked with protecting the Golden Wood, the Galadhrim will give their all to ensure its protection – for should Lothlórien fall, then Middle-earth shall surely follow…
