Théodred, Prince of Rohan ........................................................85 points

The only son of Théoden, Théodred is the rightful heir to the throne of Rohan. A highly skilled and strong warrior, Théodred has commanded Rohan’s forces across numerous battles and proven his worth many times over. Yet the prince is reckless, brash and lacks the wisdom that comes with experience, and will often charge headlong into battle without taking the time to plan a strategy. It is this overconfidence in his own abilities that will ultimately seal his fate at the hands of Saruman’s armies at the Fords of Isen.
