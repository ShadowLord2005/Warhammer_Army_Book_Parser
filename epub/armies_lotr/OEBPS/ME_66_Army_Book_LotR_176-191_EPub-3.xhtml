Freca is the Lord of the West-march during the reign of Helm Hammerhand. A hefty and cruel Man of Dunlendish heritage, Freca is known across Rohan for his arrogance and his pride. Freca’s overriding ambition is to secure the throne of Rohan for his son. Daringly, in front of the other Lords of Rohan, Freca demands that Wulf marry Héra; an act that only serves to anger Helm – and results in a brawl outside the hall of Meduseld that leads to Freca’s untimely death.
