Though the majority of Orc Captains within Mordor’s armies have gained their position through their strength and savagery, the same is not true for Gothmog’s Enforcer. Instead, it is this Orc’s unwavering loyalty and subservience to Gothmog that has seen him gain the post he now holds, relaying his master’s commands across the battlefield with authority. Whether his loyalty to Gothmog is out of admiration, or more likely cowardice, remains unknown, though it is likely of little relevance to Gothmog.

Gothmog’s Right Hand Passive – Gothmog’s Enforcer will often be found behind the lines at the side of his commander, barking Gothmog’s orders across the battlefield.

Whilst Gothmog’s Enforcer is within 3" of Gothmog, then Gothmog can spend Gothmog’s Enforcer’s Will Points to declare a Heroic Move or Heroic March instead of using his own Might Points. Additionally, when Gothmog declares a Heroic Move or Heroic March within 3" of Gothmog’s Enforcer, Gothmog can measure the range of the With Me or At the Double from Gothmog’s Enforcer rather than himself if he wishes.

Guritz, Master of Reserves .......65 points

A canny and calculating Orc, Guritz is a veteran of many wars and has gained a great deal of experience fighting against the armies of Gondor. Guritz is tasked with marshalling the reserve force at the Battle of Pelennor Fields, directing Sauron’s allies to where they are most needed to crush the Dark Lord’s enemies. It is these skills that Guritz plans to use to direct the Corsairs of Umbar towards the White City, plans that are ultimately unravelled by the Three Hunters and the Army of the Dead.
