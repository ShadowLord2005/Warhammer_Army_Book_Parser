Once the head of the Council of the Wise, Saruman the White allowed himself to be corrupted by the persuasive powers of the Dark Lord as he gazed deep into the Palantír in search of wisdom and knowledge. In doing so, Saruman became a twisted form of his previous self; no longer a wise and noble Istari but instead a tyrannical and cruel being who would use his mastery of magic to bend others to his will. What Saruman hoped to gain through his betrayal of the free peoples is unknown but he nonetheless turned his sorcerous powers against the likes of Gandalf, demanded the forest of Fangorn be ripped down and sentenced thousands of Rohirrim to die by fire and sword as his Uruk-hai rampaged across their lands.

Voice of Curunír Active – Saruman’s voice is perhaps his greatest power, with him able to be heard over great distances.

At the beginning of the game, after both sides have deployed, Saruman may choose a single enemy Hero. The chosen Hero suffers a -1 penalty to any Resist Tests they take when targeted by a Magical Power Cast by Saruman; though a natural 6 will still count as a 6.
