Frodo Baggins ............................55 points

When the One Ring was passed down to him by Bilbo, Frodo found himself thrust into an adventure of his own. Though he was only meant to take the Ring to the safety of Rivendell, it soon became apparent that Frodo’s part in the tale was not yet over. It is at the Council of Elrond that Frodo calls upon every ounce of courage he possesses and takes it upon himself to take the Ring to Mordor, into the very fires of Mount Doom. Though he may not be a fearsome warrior, Frodo more than shows his worth over his arduous journey, proving that even the smallest creatures can indeed change the course of history.
