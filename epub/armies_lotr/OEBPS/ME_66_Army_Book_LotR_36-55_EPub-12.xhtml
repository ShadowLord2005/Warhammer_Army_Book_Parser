The most skilled warriors in Rohan are drafted into the royal guard and given the duty of protecting the king and the royal line of Rohan. Handpicked for their skill-at-arms, the Rohan Royal Guard spend their time further honing their skill with a blade to ensure that they can match any foe who threatens their liege. These warriors form the spearhead of Rohan’s armies, fighting without fear at the side of their king, their polished mail and pointed spears glinting in the sun.
