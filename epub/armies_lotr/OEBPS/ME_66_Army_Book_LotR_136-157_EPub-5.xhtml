The Witch-king’s very presence on the battlefield fills his enemies with a deep sense of despair, one that can make even the boldest of hearts tremble with fear. Amongst all the evils in Middle-earth, none, save Sauron himself, is more feared than the Witch-king – the one they say no living man can kill.
