The Ents have roamed the forests of Middle-earth for uncountable years, protecting the forests from darkness and harm and keeping them places of nature and tranquillity where they can. Though they have become a rare sight by the closing years of the Third Age, and reside almost solely within the forest of Fangorn, the Ents are no less formidable. These huge, tree-like beings are more than capable of tearing down fortresses, smashing aside battlelines, and breaking apart rock and stone should they be called to war.
