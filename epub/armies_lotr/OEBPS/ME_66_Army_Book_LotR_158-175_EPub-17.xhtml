Wild Man Oathmaker .............55 points

When Saruman called upon the Wild Men of Dunland to join him in his fight against Rohan, it was one of their chieftains that answered the White Wizard’s call. Swearing to fight for Saruman even to the death, this chieftain soon became known as the Oathmaker within the Dunlending armies. Determined to enact their vengeance on the Rohirrim whom they harbour a deep, burning hatred for, the Wild Man Oathmaker will lead the Dunlending tribes with an unbridled fury.

Special Rules

Fearless, Hatred (Rohan)

Bloodoath Passive – Having sworn in blood to Saruman, the Oathmaker and his Wild Men will fight without fear in the name of the White Hand.

Friendly Dunland models within 6" of the Wild Man Oathmaker count as having the Fearless special rule.

“We will die for Saruman” Passive – With the lands of Rohan promised to them by Saruman, the Wild Men of Dunland will give their lives to wage war for the Wizard.

Whilst Saruman is alive and on the battlefield, the Wild Man Oathmaker and friendly Dunland models must re-roll To Wound Rolls of a natural 1 when making Strikes.
