Lord Thorne of the Wold is one of the many lords of Rohan under Helm’s rule. An overly extravagant looking nobleman with an ingratiating air, Lord Thorne has long served his liege in times of war and become a trusted advisor. Yet Thorne has great ambition and a lust for power, and it is these flaws that lead him to betray Rohan and join the forces of Wulf. Though he and his Men were meant to protect the east flank of Edoras, Thorne allows Wulf’s armies to pass unhindered and joins them in ransacking Edoras.
