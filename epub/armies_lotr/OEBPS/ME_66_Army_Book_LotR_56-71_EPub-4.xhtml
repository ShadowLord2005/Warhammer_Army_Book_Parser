The Men who make up the rank and file of Gondor’s forces are the backbone of her armies. Each warrior is clad in thick armour, emblazoned with the motif of the White Tree of Gondor, and armed with expertly crafted swords, shields or spears. On the battlefield, the warriors of Minas Tirith fight in disciplined battle formations designed to outclass their enemies and protect their allies. These Men have fought many battles against the armies of Mordor, all in the name of keeping the rest of Middle-earth safe from Sauron’s influence.
