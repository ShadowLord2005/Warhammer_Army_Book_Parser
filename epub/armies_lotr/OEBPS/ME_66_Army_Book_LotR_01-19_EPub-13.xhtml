The enigmatic ranger, known in the wilds as Strider, has travelled far and wide across Middle-earth fighting the servants of the Dark Lord. Having saved Frodo and his companions in Bree from the pursuit of the Black Riders, Strider has guided them to Rivendell, and there pledged his sword to Frodo in his quest to Mount Doom. An expert with both blade and bow, Strider is more than capable of holding his own against any foe that would stand against him or his allies. Yet it is at the Council of Elrond where Strider’s true lineage comes to light, revealing him not just to be a mere ranger, but Aragorn – heir to the throne of Gondor.
