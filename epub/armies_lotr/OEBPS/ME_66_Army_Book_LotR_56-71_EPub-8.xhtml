History will remember Isildur as the victor of the War of the Last Alliance, the one who took up the shards of Narsil and cut the Ring from the fingers of the Dark Lord. Yet upon Isildur taking the One Ring, the spirit of Sauron was able to latch onto him, corrupting the prince into keeping the Ring instead of destroying it. Though he kept it to be an heirloom of his kingdom, the Ring ended up betraying Isildur to his death, and the Ring was lost in the waters of the Anduin, allowing the will of Sauron to endure.

The Shards of Narsil Active – When Elendil was slain by Sauron, Isildur took up his father’s sword against the Dark Lord.

If your Army also contains Elendil, then if Elendil is slain, place a 25mm Marker where he was killed. If Isildur ends his Activation within 1" of this Marker, he may gain the Shards of Narsil – remove the Marker. The Shards of Narsil are a Unique hand weapon, and a model fighting with it in Combat gains a bonus of +1 To Wound when making Strikes.
