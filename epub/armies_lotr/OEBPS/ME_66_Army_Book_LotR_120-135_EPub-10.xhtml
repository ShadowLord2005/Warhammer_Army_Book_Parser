Special Rules

“They say a great sorceress lives in these woods…” Lothlórien is a forest filled with the subtle power of the Elves, which can render the magics of the enemy almost useless.

Friendly Lothlórien models gain the Resistant to Magic special rule.

“The Dwarf breathes so loud, we could have shot him in the dark” – The Wood Elves of Lothlórien are exceptional marksmen; especially whilst they remain hidden from view.

Friendly Lothlórien models may re-roll To Hit Rolls of a 1 when making a shooting attack if the target of the shooting attack is unable to see them due to the effects of an Elven cloak.
