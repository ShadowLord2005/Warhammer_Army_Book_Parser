Any good Corsair Captain requires a strong and loyal Bo’sun to restore order and keep their crew in line. These Men are well paid and well looked after aboard the ships, holding a position of power second only to their captain. Such favouritism is a necessity, for the captains will be acutely aware that though their Bo’sun keeps the crew in check, they will also have one eye on command of the ship should their captain falter. The commanding bellows of these Bo’suns is often enough to quell any thoughts of mutiny, for few are brave enough to stand against these brutish warriors.
