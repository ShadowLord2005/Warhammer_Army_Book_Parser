Arguably the greatest Man in the history of Middle-earth, it was Elendil who led the Men of Númenor to Eriador following the drowning of their island homeland, and he who founded the kingdoms of Gondor and Arnor. A wise and noble king, Elendil was unmatched in combat within the race of Men. When the Dark Lord threatened to plunge Middle-earth into darkness, Elendil and his sons led their people against the forces of Mordor and fought for the future of Middle-earth on the slopes of Mount Doom. Though he was tragically slain by Sauron, Elendil’s sacrifice ultimately sealed the fate of Middle-earth, protecting the free lands from the Dark Lord’s tyranny.
