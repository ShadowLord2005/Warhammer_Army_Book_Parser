Additional Rules

A Last Alliance Army must include at least one Unique Númenor Hero and at least one Unique Rivendell Hero.

Númenor Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Númenor Hero.

Rivendell Warrior models can only be included in the Warband of a Rivendell Hero.

Special Rules

Whirl of Steel – When the High Elves adopt a fighting stance, those who attempt to charge them will be met with a wall of flashing steel as the blades of the Elves cut through them.

Whenever a friendly Rivendell Infantry model that is not Engaged in Combat is Charged, they may roll a D6. On a natural 6, the model that Charged them immediately suffers a Wound. If this model was a Cavalry model, roll a further D6. On a 1-3 it will be the Mount that suffers the Wound, on a 4+ it will be the rider that suffers the Wound. This is considered an Active ability.

“There were some who resisted” – Knowing that they are Middle-earth’s last hope to stop the tyranny of Sauron, the alliance of Men and Elves will give their all against the hordes of Mordor.

Friendly models may re-roll any dice that roll a 1 when making Courage Tests.

Strength of Númenor – With their allies at their side, the Men of Númenor will muster every ounce of strength they can against the Dark Lord’s armies.

Whilst being Supported by or Supporting a friendly Rivendell model, friendly Númenor models may re-roll To Wound Rolls of a natural 1 when making Strikes.
