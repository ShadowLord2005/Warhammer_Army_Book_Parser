The Great Eagles are ancient beings, though some of them are still young by their own standards. The fledgelings have yet to reach full maturity and can be spotted by the white under feathers they still have within their majestic plumes. The more white feathers that are visible, the younger the fledgeling is. Yet this doesn’t make them any less deadly, and even a fledgeling Great Eagle is easily capable of tearing through their enemies with frightening ease.

Soaring high above the realms and kingdoms of Middle-earth, the Great Eagles of the Misty Mountains hold themselves apart from the world below. Yet in times of great need, the Eagles will still flock down to aid the free peoples, using their huge beaks and razor-sharp claws to carve through enemy armour and flesh with remarkable ease, attacking from above before circling round for another assault.
