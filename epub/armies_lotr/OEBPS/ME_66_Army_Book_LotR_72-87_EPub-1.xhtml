Even by the standards of the Elves, Círdan is immensely old, his life stretching back to before the First Age of Middle-earth. During the latter stages of the Third Age, Círdan resides in the port of the Grey Havens, tasked with looking after the ships that set sail to the Undying Lands. Over the years, Círdan has witnessed many beings leave the shores of Middle-earth, from his own kin to even the bearers of the rings of power. Should he be called upon to go to war, Círdan’s fighting days are far behind him, though he still possesses a mastery of magic far greater than most of the Elves.
