The Men of Númenor were amongst the greatest warriors of their kind and amongst the first to stand against Sauron when he launched his assaults on the free lands. Stronger than the Men of later ages, clad in sable-coloured robes and armed with the best steel-forged swords of the age, these courageous warriors marched upon Mordor and Mount Doom in a final bid to bring down the Dark Lord.
