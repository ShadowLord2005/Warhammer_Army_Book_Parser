The High King of the Elves at the close of the Second Age, Gil-galad was the greatest ruler and warrior of his kind, his wisdom and leadership inspiring his followers to great deeds of valour. It was Gil-galad who forged the Last Alliance with Elendil, marching upon Mordor for the freedom of Middle-earth. Such was Gil-galad’s bravery that he stood toe-to-toe with the Dark Lord himself, wielding his great Elven spear, Aiglos. Though he would not live to see the Dark Lord defeated, for he perished at Sauron’s hands, the name of Gil-galad lived on in songs and legend, honouring the great sacrifice of the Elven king.
