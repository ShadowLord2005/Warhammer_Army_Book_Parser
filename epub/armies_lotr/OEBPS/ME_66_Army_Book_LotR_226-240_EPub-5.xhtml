Usurpers of Edoras

During the reign of Helm Hammerhand, Lord Freca presided over the lands of the West-march. A conniving Man said to be of Dunlendish blood, Freca was set upon securing the throne of Rohan for his descendants and planned to marry his son, Wulf, to Héra, the daughter of Helm, in order to accomplish it. It is these machinations that ultimately led to the confrontation between Helm and Freca; one that ended up costing Freca his life, and leading to his son and allies being exiled from Rohan.

It is these perceived injustices and a desire for vengeance that led Wulf to raise an army in secret, one made up of his father’s allies and those who share the same blood that was said to run in his veins. Every bit his father’s son, Wulf commands his armies with an iron fist, playing off the Hill Tribesmen’s deep hatred for the Rohirrim and promising them the chance to enact revenge upon those who allegedly drove them from their homes many decades ago.

At Wulf’s side stands General Targg, his most trusted advisor, who also provided counsel to Freca in years gone by, and a warrior of great experience having fought many battles under the banners of his lords. Yet there are more than just the Hill Tribes in the armies raised against Edoras, for not every Man of Rohan is loyal to their king, and there may be some that will betray Helm Hammerhand for the promise of riches and power.

The armies of Dunland are filled with warriors who have been whipped into a blood-crazed battle frenzy by their leaders, ready to unleash their full might upon the kingdom of Rohan with no thought for their own well-being. Under the direction of Wulf, the Hill Tribesmen will leave none of their enemy’s leaders alive, slaughtering them where they stand as an example of the might of Wulf and his father. So great are the amassed armies of the Hill Tribesmen, that even the mighty Helm Hammerhand may not be able to fend off their assault.
