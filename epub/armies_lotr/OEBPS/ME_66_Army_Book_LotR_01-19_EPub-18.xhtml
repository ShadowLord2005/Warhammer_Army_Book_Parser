During the closing years of the Third Age, Bilbo’s 111th birthday is approaching and his life of adventure is behind him. Many years have passed since his journey with the company of Thorin Oakenshield and his finding of the magic ring in Gollum’s cave, though time has been kind to Bilbo. Though Bilbo is unaware of the fact he carries the One Ring itself, the truth is that the Ring has preserved Bilbo against old age. Yet in doing so, over the course of the next 60 years the Ring has begun to finally take hold of Bilbo, and if he is unable to finally let go of it then it may consume him entirely.
